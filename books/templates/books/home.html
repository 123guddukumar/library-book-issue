About Library Owner

Guddu Kumar

Guddu Kumar is the passionate founder of our library, with over 10 years of experience in promoting literacy and education. His vision is to create a community space where knowledge is accessible to all. When not managing the library, Guddu enjoys reading historical novels and mentoring young readers.
